HISTORY OF DULIKHULU PRIMARY SCHOOL

Dulikhululu primary school is a public state primary school located at Emdletsheni tribal Authority, KwaZulu Natal at Ekhanyakude Municipality. Since the school open the doors, it has been a place where all students in KwaZulu Natal and beyond can call home as it celebrates and embrace ethic and cultural diversity. The school is classified under quintile 1 as a non-fee school. The school helps children to learn in a diverse way student develop social, behavioural, emotional as well as skills of diversity. it teaches isizulu as home language and English as a first additional language
